ABOUT US

Soil Tech Global Corporation is a newly found Geotechnical Company that offers advanced hydraulic fed drilling rigs in the Philippines. 

The company was founded on the month of May, year 2022. The first project handled and operated in the Philippines was the Laguna Lakeshore Road Network Project – Phase 1, from Lower Bicutan, Taguig to Calamba, Laguna.

Vision

To provide quality and efficient geotechnical services to our partners and clients.

Mission

To deliver environment friendly, safe and time efficient drilling programs for the betterment of our projects.

Projects

Laguna Lakeshore Road Network – Phase 1

With a main alignment of 37.60 kilometers, the Laguna Lakeshore Road Network Project, Phase I includes eight (8) interchanges in Sucat, Alabang, Tunasan, San Pedro/Binan, Santa Rosa, Cabuyao, and Calamba.

This two-lane primary road is made up of an 11.80-kilometer viaduct connecting Lower Bicutan to Muntinlupa and a 25.80-kilometer combination of onshore viaduct and embankment connecting Muntinlupa to Calamba.
